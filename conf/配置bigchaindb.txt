
1. 安装NTP

    sudo  apt-get install  ntp  ,  参考 http://bigchaindb.readthedocs.io/en/latest/appendices/ntp-notes.html

 2. rethinkdb配置

    参考    配置rethinkdb.txt

3. 修改bigchaindb配置文件 , $HOME/.bigchaindb

    Change "keyring": [] to "keyring": ["public_key_of_other_node_A", "public_key_of_other_node_B", "..."] i.e. a list of the public keys of all the other nodes in the federation. The keyring should not include your node’s public key.

    参考 http://bigchaindb.readthedocs.io/en/latest/nodes/setup-run-node.html#configure-bigchaindb-server
